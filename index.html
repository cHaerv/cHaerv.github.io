Orbis Terrarum

class: Planet and Habitat

mass: 1.53 Earths

This gas Giant has not been explored due to the volatile nature of its habitat. It is believed that this was some form of offworld colony due to the drift of Ignotum prime closer to its Star

The habitat appears to be self contained and orbits around the planet to harvest its resources but is as of now innoperable.

Bellum Scissum

class: Planet

mass: 0.078 Earths

This planet may resemble mars but was thought to have been terraformed once before due to its natural deposits of carbon layers indicating an agricultural society.

Many fragments of Thorium-231 have been found along with Lead-207 possibly indicating a large scale use of fission or maybe even radioactive shelling on the planets surface.
